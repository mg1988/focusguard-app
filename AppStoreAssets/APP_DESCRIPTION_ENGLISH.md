## App Description (Optimized for English Markets)

```
FocusGuard is an intelligent focus assistant for macOS that uses AI to keep you productive and healthy. Unlike traditional focus apps, it actively monitors your attention and posture in real-time.

PRIVACY FIRST
- All processing happens locally on your device
- AI facial recognition runs entirely in memory
- No images are ever stored or uploaded
- No account required, works out of the box

SMART FOCUS DETECTION
- Real-time attention monitoring using Apple Vision
- Distraction alerts when you look away from screen
- Drowsiness detection to keep you alert
- Adjustable sensitivity: Low (5s), Medium (3s), High (1s)
- Progressive notification system: sound, haptic, banner

POSTURE HEALTH MONITOR
- Detects poor posture: slouching, leaning, wrong distance
- Three-stage progressive reminders (3s, 10s, 20s)
- Prevents back pain and promotes healthy work habits
- Optional posture detection with customizable alerts

FOCUS STATISTICS & ANALYTICS
- 7-day focus heatmap visualization
- Track focus duration, distractions, and drowsiness
- Efficiency score to measure productivity
- Export data to CSV for detailed analysis

SMART SNAPSHOTS
- Auto-capture when distracted or drowsy
- Review what caused your focus breaks
- All photos stored locally, full control
- Easy snapshot management and deletion

CUSTOMIZATION
- Forward timer or countdown modes
- Set focus goals and track progress
- Launch at login for continuous protection
- Do Not Disturb mode: auto-pause during fullscreen apps
- Multi-language support (11 languages)
- Dark/Light mode automatic adaptation

PERFECT FOR
- Remote workers and digital nomads
- Students and online learners
- Developers and designers
- Writers and creative professionals
- Anyone struggling with focus

WHY FOCUSGUARD?
Most focus apps just block distractions. FocusGuard actively monitors your attention and provides real-time feedback. It's like having a personal productivity coach that watches over your shoulder.

The AI-powered detection uses Apple's Vision framework to analyze facial landmarks and eye movement, ensuring accurate and responsive monitoring without compromising your privacy.

SYSTEM REQUIREMENTS
- macOS 13.0 or later
- Camera access for face detection
- Works with built-in or external cameras

DOWNLOAD NOW
Take control of your focus and build better work habits. FocusGuard helps you stay on track, one session at a time.

SUPPORT & FEEDBACK
We're constantly improving FocusGuard. Send your feedback, feature requests, or bug reports to help us make it better.
```
