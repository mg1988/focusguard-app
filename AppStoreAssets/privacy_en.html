🔒 Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 9, 2026

1. Introduction

FocusGuard ("we", "our", or "this app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

2. Information Collection and Use

2.1 Local Processing Principle

FocusGuard follows a 100% local processing principle:

  • ✅ All facial recognition and data analysis are performed locally on your device
  • ✅ Image data is never stored on disk
  • ✅ Data is never uploaded to any server
  • ✅ No internet connection required for core features

2.2 Camera Access

  • What we collect: Facial images for real-time analysis
  • Purpose: Detect focus state, distractions, drowsiness, and posture
  • Storage: Images are processed temporarily in memory and discarded immediately after analysis
  • Snapshot feature: When enabled by the user, photos are stored locally on your device and can be deleted at any time

2.3 Notification Permissions

  • Purpose: Send focus reminders, distraction alerts, drowsiness alerts, and posture reminders
  • Data: Does not include personally identifiable information

2.4 Local Data Storage

The app stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Focus duration statistics
  • Distraction/drowsiness count records
  • Captured photos (if enabled)
  • Posture history records
  • User settings and preferences

All data is stored only on your device and is never synced to the cloud or third-party servers.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share any of your personal information with third parties.

Exceptions only apply in the following circumstances:

  • Legal requirements: As required by law or judicial proceedings
  • Rights protection: To protect our rights or the safety of others

4. Data Security

We take appropriate security measures to protect your data:

  • All data processing is completed locally
  • No data is transmitted to external servers
  • No third-party analytics SDKs are integrated
  • No advertising tracking code is included

5. Children's Privacy

FocusGuard is suitable for users of all ages, including children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Third-Party Links

This app may contain links to third-party websites or services (such as technical support websites). We are not responsible for the privacy policies of these third-party websites.

7. Data Deletion

You can delete the app at any time to clear all local data:

  • Captured photos
  • Statistics data
  • User settings

After deleting the app, all data will be permanently deleted and cannot be recovered.

8. Privacy Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated policies will be published within the app and on our website.
